SubGuard is a privacy-first subscription tracker that helps you manage all your recurring expenses in one place. Know exactly what you're paying for, when renewals are due, and how much you're spending.

Features:
• Track all subscriptions and recurring payments
• Renewal reminders before you're charged
• Monthly and annual spending summaries
• Categorize subscriptions by type
• 100% private — no account, no cloud sync required
• Multi-currency support
