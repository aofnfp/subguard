## Full Description

Take control of your subscriptions with SubGuard — a simple, private subscription tracker that helps you see exactly where your money goes every month.

**See the Full Picture**
SubGuard gives you a clear overview of all your active subscriptions, how much you're spending monthly and annually, and when each renewal is coming up. No more surprise charges.

**Key Features:**
- Track unlimited subscriptions with cost, billing cycle, and category
- Dashboard showing total monthly and annual spending
- Renewal reminders so you never miss a cancellation window
- Category breakdown to see where your money goes
- Dark mode and light mode support
- Works completely offline — your data never leaves your device

**Premium Features ($1.99/mo or $14.99/yr):**
- Advanced spending analytics and trends
- Custom categories and tags
- Ad-free experience
- Export subscription data

SubGuard is built with privacy first. All your financial data stays on your device — we never see it, store it, or share it.
